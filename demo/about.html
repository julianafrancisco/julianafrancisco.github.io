About Me


Juliana was born in Brooklyn, New York in 1993. She graduated high school in 2011 and spent 8 years as a political organizer, working in voter education and in New York City, as well as Oregan, Arizona, Florida, Maine, and Pennsylvania.

She enrolled in BMCC during the Fall of 2019 and is currently in her final semester. She is learning Cinema 3D, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, inDesign, and Audacity. She is currently majoring in Animation and Motion Graphics and hopes to become an Animator or Video Game Designer.

She is currently quarantined and stressed out due to "Distance Learning" but she keeps her spirits high by playing Stardew Valley, Phasmophobia, and the Sims 3.
